Namami
Gange

Namami Gange Programme is an Integrated Conservation Mission,
approved as a Flagship Programme by the Union Government of
India in June 2014 with a budget outlay
of Rs. 22,500 crore from 2023-26 to accomplish the twin
objectives of effective abatement of pollution, conservation
and rejuvenation of National River Ganga.
